Air Condition Tutor - 1 TR

Model:ACL-01

Description:

This laboratory-scale working model is mounted on a durable M.S. square tube frame with wheels and features a powder-coated instrumentation panel. It is designed to:

  • Calculate Co-efficient of Performance (COP)
  • Evaluate Tonnage Capacity
